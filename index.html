About WashU Bhangra

We are WashU Bhangra (aka WUBB), one of the premier South Asian cultural dance groups on campus. Our dance style originates in the state of Punjab, India but has spread all across the globe. Bhangra is known for its high energy, colorful sets, fun, and pure swag.

During the year we perform both competitively around the nation and right here on campus! In recent years we have competed in Toronto, ON, Chicago, IL, Madison, WI, and Seattle, WA. We also perform at on-campus events like WU Best Dance Crew, WU Dance Off, Relay for Life, Celebrations weekend, and many more!
